<p>As we celebrate 25 years of excellence, we honour the strong foundations
that have shaped our journey and renew our commitment to nurturing the
next generation of Learners, Thinkers and Leaders. Guided by our enduring
JW Spirit, we stride forward with confidence — transforming youthful aspirations
into enduring legacies that will define the future of Jurong West Secondary
School.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The tagline “Youthful, Excellence, Horizons” reflects both who we are
and who we aspire to be. Youthful captures the vibrancy and boundless possibilities
that energise our community. Excellence goes beyond achievements to embrace
character, growth and agency, with each and every student and staff member
committing to strengthen the JW identity and Spirit. Horizons expresses
our forward-looking vision of the school — empowering students to engage
the wider community, innovate with purpose, and shape the next 25 years
of JWSS’s journey.</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>What the Logo Means</strong>
</p>
<p>The 25th anniversary logo draws inspiration from tangram geometry, where
individual shapes come together to form a meaningful whole. This reflects
our JW community, diverse individuals united by shared values and purpose.</p>
<p>It presents a confident, celebratory spirit, with the “25” seamlessly
integrated with an abstract eagle. Its uplifted wings and forward-leaning
form symbolise resilience, courage and aspiration — hallmarks of the JW
Spirit.</p>
<p>Each angular facet represents milestones and contributions across our
25year journey, while the balance of warm and cool tones reflects harmony
between heritage and innovation. Together, the logo tells the story of
a community that honours its past and rises boldly toward the future.</p>
