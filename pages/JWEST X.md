<h1><strong>LEAD UP to JWEST-X</strong></h1>

<p></p>
<p>As part of our 25th Anniversary Celebrations, we warmly invite you to
take part in the JWest-X Virtual Challenge — a collective fitness goal
that captures our JW Spirit: a school brimming with youthful energy, a
relentless pursuit of excellence, and a community always looking toward
new horizons.</p>
<p>&nbsp;🎯 <strong>OUR COLLECTIVE GOAL</strong>
</p>
<p>As a school, we aim to cover <strong>25,000 km</strong> across 5 weeks —
a distance that mirrors our 25 years of shared milestones, friendships,
and growth as one community.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>📅 <strong>CHALLENGE PERIOD</strong>
</p>
<p>27 March 2026 (Friday) to 1 May 2026 (Friday)</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>🏅 <strong>ACCEPTED ACTIVITIES</strong>
</p>
<p>Walking, running, or cycling —outdoors or in the gym (on treadmills and
cycling machines included). For gym sessions, please include a screenshot
or photo of yourself during the activity as record of participation.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>📱 <strong>ACCEPTED APPS</strong>
</p>
<p>You may record your distances using any of the following fitness apps:</p>
<p>• Strava (primary app)</p>
<p>• Nike Run Club</p>
<p>• Adidas Running</p>
<p>• Couch to 5K</p>
<p>• Runkeeper</p>
<p>• MapMyRun</p>
<p>• Garmin Connect</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>📝 SUBMISSION</strong>
</p>
<p>Please submit your logged distances via: <a href="https://go.gov.sg/jws25thleadup" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/jws25thleadup</a>.
Weekly results will be published via the school website.</p>
