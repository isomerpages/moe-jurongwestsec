<p>As we celebrate 25 years of excellence, we honour the strong foundations
that have shaped our journey while renewing our commitment to nurturing
the next generation of Learners, Thinkers and Leaders. Guided by our enduring
JW Spirit, we stride forward with confidence and conviction — transforming
youthful aspirations into enduring legacies that will define the future
of Jurong West Secondary School.</p>
<p>The tagline <strong>“Youthful, Excellence, Horizons”</strong> captures both
our identity and our aspiration. <em>Youthful</em> reflects the vibrancy,
courage and boundless possibility that energise our school community and
infuse our experiences with purpose and passion. <em>Excellence</em> is not
merely about achievement, but about character, growth and agency — where
every staff member and student takes ownership in strengthening our school
identity, deepening the JW Spirit, and cultivating a shared sense of pride
and belonging. <em>Horizons</em> speaks to our forward-looking vision: empowering
students to engage the wider community, pioneer innovative ideas, and reimagine
possibilities that will shape the next 25 years of JWSS’s journey.</p>
<p><strong>What the logo means</strong>
</p>
<p>The 25th anniversary logo presents a confident and celebratory feel where
the “25” and abstract eagle are seamlessly integrated. The uplifted wings
and forward-leaning geometry express resilience, courage, and aspiration,
the core attributes of the JW Spirit.</p>
<p>Each angular facet symbolises a milestone, experience, and contribution
that has shaped the school over the past 25 years. The interplay of warm
and cool colours creates visual balance, symbolising harmony between tradition
and innovation.</p>
<p>It tells a story of a community that has moved the earth and touched the
sky, honouring the past while confidently embracing the future.</p>
