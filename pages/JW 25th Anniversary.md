<p>As we celebrate 25 years of excellence, we honour the strong foundations
that have shaped our journey and renew our commitment to nurturing the
next generation of Learners, Thinkers and Leaders. Guided by our enduring
JW Spirit, we stride forward with confidence — transforming youthful aspirations
into enduring legacies that will define the future of Jurong West Secondary
School.</p>
<p>The tagline <strong>“Youthful, Excellence, Horizons”</strong> reflects both
who we are and who we aspire to be. <em>Youthful</em> captures the vibrancy
and boundless possibility that energise our community. <em>Excellence</em> goes
beyond achievement to embrace character, growth and agency, as every staff
member and student strengthens our identity and deepens the JW Spirit. <em>Horizons</em> expresses
our forward-looking vision — empowering students to engage the wider community,
innovate with purpose, and shape the next 25 years of JWSS’s journey.</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>What the Logo Means</strong>
</p>
<p>The 25th anniversary logo presents a confident, celebratory spirit, with
the “25” seamlessly integrated with an abstract eagle. Its uplifted wings
and forward-leaning form symbolise resilience, courage and aspiration —
hallmarks of the JW Spirit.</p>
<p>Each angular facet represents milestones and contributions across our
25-year journey, while the balance of warm and cool tones reflects harmony
between heritage and innovation. Together, the logo tells the story of
a community that honours its past and rises boldly toward the future.</p>
