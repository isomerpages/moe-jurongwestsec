</p>
<p><strong><u>A. INTRODUCTION</u></strong>
</p>

for Secondary One admission in 2025 with talents in various areas to apply
for Direct School Admission to Secondary One in 2024.</p>

character development are pillars of a holistic education which make our

<p><strong><u>D. APPLICATION PROCEDURE</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Centralised DSA-Sec Application Process:</p>
<p>Application for DSA-Sec can be submitted through the online DSA-Sec Portal.
The application is free-of-charge and will be open from 4 May 2023 to 31
May 2023. For more details, please refer to MOE website – <a href="http://www.moe.gov.sg/dsa-sec" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">www.moe.gov.sg/dsa-sec</a>.</p>
<p><strong><u>E. SELECTION PROCESS BY SCHOOL</u></strong>
</p>
<p>All shortlisted students will be informed latest by <strong><u>7 Jul 2023</u></strong> to
attend an interview, audition, sports trial or visual art aptitude test
depending on the DSA area.</p>
<p>Students who are shortlisted to attend the interview and trials are to
report to the school’s General Office where the teacher overseeing the
DSA Exercise will meet them to give further instructions.

<p><strong><u>F. RELEASE OF OUTCOME OF DSA-SEC EXERCISE</u></strong>
</p>
<p>All applicants would be informed of their selection outcome by 11 Sep
2023 [i.e. Confirmed Offer (CO), Wait List (WL) or Unsuccessful].</p>
<p>Applicants given CO are guaranteed admission only if they:
<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Indicate the school during School Preference Submission (23 Oct – 27 Oct
2023)</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Qualify for an academic course offered by the school (i.e. G3, G2, G1)
based on PSLE results.</p>
<p></p>
<p>DSA-Sec allocation results will be released through Primary Schools together
with PSLE results.
<br>
</p>
<p>Please note that students successfully allocated to their DSA-Sec schools
will <strong><u>NOT</u> </strong>be allowed to participate in the annual
S1 Posting Exercise to opt for secondary schools, and are expected to honour
the commitment to the allocated DSA-Sec school for the entire duration
of the programme. They will also <strong><u>NOT</u> </strong>be allowed
to transfer to another school after the release of the PSLE results.
<br>
</p>
<p>Students who are successfully admitted to the school via DSA are expected
to honour their commitment to the school, and participate in the activities
related to the talent they are selected for from year 1-4.</p>
<p><strong><u>G. ENQUIRIES</u></strong>
</p>
<p>The selection criteria and process for the talent areas will be updated
from early-May. For more information on DSA-Sec, please refer to the MOE
website at https://www.moe.gov.sg/dsa-sec.</p>
<p>Parents and students are encouraged to choose schools wisely based on
the student’s aptitudes and strengths, bearing in mind the schools’ academic
and non-academic requirements, and the programmes available to develop
the area of talent.
